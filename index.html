ABOUT THE CITY

Godda District is one among 24 Districts of Jharkhand State,India. It is is Located 297 KM west towards State capital Ranchi. It is 12th Largest District in the State by population santali and Hindi is the Local Language here. Godda District is divided into 8 Blocks , 211 Panchayats , 1325 Villages.
